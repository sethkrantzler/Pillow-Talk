Last Night's Sleep

Here are some ways to improve your sleep:

Before Bed

  • Don’t use your cellphone or other electronics for an hour before going to sleep
  • Go to bed and wake up around the same time every day
  • Try not to eat a big meal to close to sleeping
  • Dim the lights around your home in the hours leading up to sleep
  • Try to clear your mind of all the day’s stresses in the hours leading up to sleep

    During the Day

  • If you have to nap, try to keep them under twenty minutes
  • Try not to drink any caffeinated drinks after the morning
  • Exercise for around an hour during the day
